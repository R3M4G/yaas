What is this?

youtube-dl is an open-source video downloading tool that can download videos from a wide variety of sites. It's pretty cool.

yaas (youtube-dl as a service) provides a really simple front-end to youtube-dl.

Supported sites

  • youtube.com
  • vimeo.com
  • twitch.tv
  • soundcloud.com
  • instagram.com
  • dailymotion.com
  • cnn.com
